About

This application was initiated through the Utah Broadband Project. The Utah Broadband Project is a part of the SBI grants funded by NTIA. The SBI program, led by states, works to facilitate the integration of broadband and information technology into state and local economies. Through the SBI program, NTIA and the FCC maintain the National Broadband Map, based on data collected from states.

Address points are used by the Utah Broadband Project, as well as other projects, to create a more accurate inventory of known address locations in support of broadband accessibility analysis, public safety applications and many other uses. This address point editing platform allows data stewards to maintain their address point data in an efficient, browser-based process.
